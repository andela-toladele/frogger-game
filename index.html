FROGGER GAME: ABOUT

Try to move the player to the other side of the board with the ARROW KEYS without hitting the beetles. Each time you hit the rock on the other side of the board your score increases. The speed increases based on the number of times you get to the other side of the board. The game starts with 3 lives, hit the diamond gem each time it appears to have 1 added to the life count. Each time you hit any of the beetles, the life decreases by 1. Good luck!
